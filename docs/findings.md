# Findings Log

## Research
- **Branding:** User research indicates that the mint color conveys trust and accessibility, while orange projects professional energy and business growth for lawyers.
- **Competitor Analysis:** Most legal marketplaces have complex flows. LegalPath's 3-step publication is a significant UX advantage.
- **Technology:** React 19 provides the necessary stability and performance for a highly interactive platform.

## Technical Constraints
- **Supabase Integration:** Requires careful RLS policy design to maintain absolute user anonymity.
- **Mobile Responsiveness:** Floating UI elements must be carefully managed to avoid clashes with text on smaller screens.
